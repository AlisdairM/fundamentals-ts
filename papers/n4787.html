Change history and motions

There have been two motions since the previous revision:

  • San Diego 2018, LWG Motion 2: Move to apply to the Library Fundamentals TS working paper the proposed resolution of issues 2960 and 3134 in P1224R0 (C++ Standard Library Issues to be moved in San Diego).
  • San Diego 2018, LWG Motion 3: Move to apply to the Library Fundamentals TS working paper the proposed wording in P1210R0 (Completing the Rebase of Library Fundamentals, Version 3, Working Draft). This also resolves LWG issue 3134.

Editorial changes

The feature test macro table has been reordered to match the clause structure.
